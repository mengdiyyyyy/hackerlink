### Personal Background

I am originally from Wuhan, a beautiful city known for the Yangtze River and Yellow Crane Tower. When I was 18, I went to Beijing for a Bachelor’s degree. After graduation, I worked for Ernest Young as an auditor. After that, I got married rotated in my family business among several departments in Jincheng in Shanxi Province and Chi Zhou in Anhui Province. My family business manufactures cast iron drainage pipes and ductile iron pipes. In the future, I plan to provide the best drainage solutions to all households.

### Career Progress to Date

I have rotated among several positions in my family enterprise. In the beginning, I was the production planning supervisor. I was responsible for setting production plans and tracking the progress of each order. I organized the production department, supply department, and quality department to evaluate special requests and increased order on-time delivery rate from 85% to 95%. Then, I rotated to the finance department. I am responsible for cost management, working capital management as well as financial budgeting and forecasting. I classified accounts receivables into 5 types and collected overdue receivables quickly. By doing this, I reduced working capital by more than 30 million. After that, I went to the Anhui plant and worked as the operating supervisor. I saved more than 10 million costs annually after tracking the usage of primary materials and revising standard operating procedures (SOPs) of critical positions such as centrifuge operators.

Now I have taken the position of operating director for 2 years. I designed and applied budget management patterns in 3 SBUs using activity-based costing. I compared data timely, distinguished wastes, and carried on the improvement to it, to increase the profitability of each SBU.

### Career goals

In the short term, I will return to my family business and hold the position of general manager of one division after graduation. I plan to reorganize the operating team and apply the technical skills and managerial theories into practice to improve the market penetration of our products. In the long term, our group will build a new factory in a local industrial park. I am determined to achieve a first-class pipe manufacturing factory in the world. I plan to design new production lines for pipes with new coatings. I really want to make some difference to help promote direct drinking water in China.

More importantly, I hope we will no longer be bothered by the return odor or the noises from our restrooms. To achieve this, I will cooperate with more universities and design institutes and make full use of our national drainage experimental tower. I will spare no effort to provide the best drainage solutions to all households.

The "Made in China 2025" initiative is a strategic plan to upgrade the manufacturing capabilities and quality of Chinese products, aiming to compete with German and Japanese manufacturing standards. Xuanshi produces cast iron drainage pipes and ductile iron pipes. We need to transition from a rapid growth model to one that is more balanced, sustainable, and driven by innovation and consumer demand.

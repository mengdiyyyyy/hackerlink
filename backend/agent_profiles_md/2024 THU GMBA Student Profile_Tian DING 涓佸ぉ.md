### Personal Background

I was born and raised in Beijing, China. Apart from two years studying abroad, I have spent most of my life in this city. I adore Beijing's historical red walls and blue tiles, as well as its modern skyscrapers. This ancient yet open-minded metropolis fills me with cultural pride and has made me a person with an open mindset, ready to embrace life's challenges.

### Career Progress to Date

In 2019, I joined the New Beijing News, a renowned media organization in China. Over the course of three years, I held positions as a new media editor and an international reporter. I was also entrusted to build a special reporting team from scratch alongside another journalist. The news stories we created gained significant attention, repeatedly making it to the trending lists on Chinese social media platforms, as well as garnering over 100,000 views on overseas platforms like Twitter and YouTube.

Starting in 2021, I joined my family's business and took over the education division. Within three years, we achieved a successful establishment of a school for grades 1-9, a kindergarten, and a childcare center. Currently, we have a total enrollment of 3,300 students and employ 260 staff members. Due to outstanding performance, I was promoted to the position of Executive Director within the parent company last year. In this role, I have been given the chance to engage with other business sectors and explore new opportunities.

### Career goals

In the short term, my career goal is to continue developing the education business by enhancing the service quality of our existing schools and institutions. My objective is to establish our brand as a leading private school in the local community and expand further by constructing a high school, all while offering a diverse range of extracurricular activities. I also hope to make some exploration in the field of education technology, especially in the field of smart education driven by artificial intelligence and big data.

Looking ahead, my long-term goal is to accumulate more professional experience and explore further industry opportunities. I aspire to lead our family business in achieving upgrades and transformations, adapting to the changing landscape and emerging trends.

### Reason(s) for doing an MBA at Tsinghua in China now

With a background rooted in the humanities, I am immensely grateful for the foundational impact it had on shaping my character, fostering critical thinking, and effective communication abilities. It provided me with the early opportunity to contemplate profound life questions. However, transitioning from my childhood aspiration of becoming a writer to becoming a businesswoman has proven to be a challenging endeavor. As I progressed in my career, I gradually recognized the vital significance of furthering my education in the fields of business and STEM. Amongst the esteemed academic institutions, Tsinghua University stands out as my optimal choice. It holds a distinguished reputation as China's most prestigious institution, renowned for its robust research capabilities, pragmatic academic atmosphere, and highly acclaimed MBA program. Tsinghua also grants access to a powerful network of alumni. This network presents the opportunity to connect with exceptional individuals from diverse backgrounds, establishing valuable friendships and paving the way for future collaborations in the business world.

### The most important thing you’ve learned (or known) about China

China, with its profoundly ancient history, has experienced a remarkable unity spanning two millennia and served as a prominent global center for an extended period. This unparalleled historical heritage forms the bedrock of Chinese culture. Notably, the Chinese elites develop a mindset that transcends localized and immediate concerns, consistently striving for universal and historically rooted solutions. This inclination, I think, is the most important key to understanding China’s intellectual history.

### Personal Background

I was born and raised in Haidian district, Beijing, and spent my teenage years in Haidian Foreign Language School. Art has been my passion since childhood. I expressed my imagination by scribbling on any surface I could find, then my grandpa sent me to art school at the age of five. I successfully applied to an art school in New York City at age 17, my first dream was to become an artist. During my first year of college, I found myself developed more interests in art business industry instead of creating, this made me became an artist agent and exhibition manager after graduation.

Additionally, I consider myself an energetic person who enjoys pursuing diverse interests. In my spare time, I have developed a passion for wine tasting and have taken WSET lessons to enhance my knowledge in this area.

### Career Progress to Date

I joined Neal Digital (2020 - 2023) as their first employee immediately after completing my studies. Over the past three years, I have progressed from an executive staff member to an exhibition manager and artist agent. I have led my team to achieve significant milestones for the company.

For example, we organized the largest physical digital art exhibitions in multiple cities in China, showcasing over 2,000 pieces, co-curated an exhibition in collaboration with government and big company, which attracted over 10,000 visitors during the May Day holiday; partnered with the major auction houses in China and hosted seven digital art auctions with an average closing rate of 70%; curated solo exhibitions for artists and secured public exhibition opportunities. We have established partnerships with over 30 commercial entities. Personally, I have built an online exhibition brand that was featured on a leading global platform for digital art sales.

### Personal Background

Unlike the laid-back atmosphere of Chengdu, I grew up immersed in the environment of business management. Perhaps it was this constant exposure that instilled in me the determination to carve out my own path through hard work. After graduating from university, I used my spare time to start my own company. This experience made me acutely aware of my shortcomings in practical management. However, I was fortunate to set clearer goals for my future growth and to meet many young and capable women professionals along the way. Moving forward, I will continue to stay true to my goals.

### Career Progress to Date

After graduating from university, I made the decisive choice to forgo the opportunity to pursue a master's degree in the UK and instead joined my family business, starting my career as an accountant. This experience gave me an in-depth understanding of the core aspects of corporate financial management. However, as I gained more work experience, I gradually realized that finance was only one facet of management. To enhance my skills more comprehensively, I shifted my focus to marketing. In my subsequent career development, I took on the role of a marketing manager, where I dedicated myself to helping our family’s traditional logistics business expand into online commerce. This shift not only significantly boosted the company's visibility in the southwestern region but also allowed me to personally experience the complexities and appeal of market operations. Through this experience, I gained valuable marketing insights and connected with like-minded partners. Collaborating with these partners led me to embark on an entirely new career path, launching my second "side business." This new venture involved exploring new business models that I had never encountered in the traditional physical industry, broadening my perspective on future development directions. This journey has not only enriched my professional experience but also provided me with more opportunities for growth.

Looking ahead, I plan to continue integrating my knowledge of finance and marketing, exploring more business possibilities, and continuously challenging myself to achieve greater career success.

### The most important thing you’ve learned (or known) about China

This blend is evident in how ancient philosophies like Confucianism continue to influence social and business practices, while at the same time, China rapidly emerges as a global leader in technology, innovation, and infrastructure. The country’s ability to preserve its cultural heritage, even as it undergoes significant transformation, showcases a unique resilience and adaptability that sets China apart on the world stage. This duality creates a dynamic environment where historical values coexist with forward-thinking ambitions, shaping a society that is both deeply connected to its past and boldly oriented towards the future.

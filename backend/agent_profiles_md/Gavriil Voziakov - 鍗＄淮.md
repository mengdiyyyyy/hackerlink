### The most important thing you’ve learned (or known) about China

The Chinese way of thinking 想法 (xiǎng fǎ) is heavily influenced by cultural context. Confucianism and the concepts of Face and Guanxi all play most important role.

Without understanding those cultural influences, a Westerner will have a hard time understanding the way Chinese communicate and behave in a business setting and in everyday life.

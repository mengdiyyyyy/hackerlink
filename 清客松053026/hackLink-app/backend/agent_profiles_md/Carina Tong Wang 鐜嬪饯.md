### Personal Background

I am a native Beijinger. I went to McGill University in Canada to pursue a degree in finance. To be honest, the winters in Eastern Canada are really cold, and as someone who loves Chinese food, I decided to return to China to work after considering the climate and the quality of food. I spent five years working in investment banking, focusing on IPO projects in Hong Kong and overseas. I witnessed the boom and the downturn of Chinese companies going public overseas. During this financial downturn, I decided to continue my education by enrolling in the MBA program at Tsinghua University.

### Career Progress to Date

Over the past five years, I’ve had two jobs, one at a foreign investment bank and the other at an internet brokerage, both in investment banking department. As the project leader, I was deeply involved in the overseas IPOs of many well-known Chinese companies, gaining extensive experience and resources related to investment, financing, and IPOs. During my time at Tiger Brokers, I not only enhanced my investment banking skills but also gained insight into the new business model of "Internet + Finance." This experience has provided fresh inspiration for my future career and industry aspirations.

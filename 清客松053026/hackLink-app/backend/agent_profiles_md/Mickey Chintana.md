### Personal Background

I was born in Phuket, a beautiful island in southern Thailand, and moved to Bangkok at the age of 12 to attend a boarding school. Growing up independently has shaped my personality, giving me a strong sense of self-awareness and determination. I pride myself on being highly adaptable, able to blend seamlessly into any situation, and I take initiative in researching and making decisions on my own.

### Career Progress to Date

Initially, I had no clear direction for my career, as the Thai education system encourages students to excel in all areas, making it challenging to pinpoint a specific path. After graduating with a degree in economics from Chulalongkorn University, I explored various fields through internships in consulting, data analysis, and management traineeships. Through these experiences, I discovered my passion for working with people and combined this with my personal management skills, which ultimately led me to become the youngest project manager at a tech company. Despite starting with no prior knowledge of team management or a tech background, I continuously learned and adapted. Now, after nearly six years in this role, I've evolved into a hybrid of project and product management. Although I'm confident in my project management abilities, I recognize that I still have room to grow, particularly in developing my business acumen.

### Career goals

I still have a deep passion for managing projects, as it allows me to bring ideas to life, lead teams towards shared goals, and create tangible impact. However, I realize that to truly excel in this role and make a broader contribution, I need to enhance my business acumen. My goal is to become a well-rounded project manager, not just proficient in execution but also equipped with the strategic insights necessary to consult for businesses. By developing these skills, I aim to lead initiatives that are not only successful but also meaningful, with the potential to drive Thailand forward in the global economy. My ambition is to play a pivotal role in shaping the future of the business landscape in my country, ensuring that the projects I manage contribute to long-term growth and innovation.

### Reason(s) for doing an MBA at Tsinghua in China now

“If not now then when?” – This was the question I posed to myself, and it led me to pursue an MBA. I have now reached a stage in my career where I need new knowledge, skills, and experiences to continue growing. While I have consistently excelled in project management, I recognize gaps in my understanding of business areas such as strategy, finance, and accounting.

This realization led me to explore MBA programs that would not only address these gaps but also align with my learning style and career aspirations. Tsinghua's emphasis on experiential learning, industry insights, and real-world case studies perfectly matches what I’m looking for. Additionally, the integration of both Chinese and global perspectives will further enrich my understanding of the business landscape, making it the ideal choice for my continued professional development.

### The most important thing you’ve learned (or known) about China

I initially thought Beijing and Shanghai were close to each other, but despite their short distance on a map, traveling between the two cities actually takes a considerable amount of time. And when zoomed out on the map, we can see how huge China is !

### Any other interesting fact about yourself

Many people have asked me how I became the youngest project manager without a technical background, how to manage projects, or which tools to use etc. In response, I created educational content on TikTok. With the growing demand for project and product management roles in the Thai tech industry, there's a noticeable knowledge gap. My channel provides practical tips, shares my experiences and offers guidance to young professionals navigating their careers. It has evolved into a thriving community, with many followers successfully securing tech positions thanks to the advice and support shared. My goal is to be a valuable resource, helping individuals take their first steps and excel in their tech careers.

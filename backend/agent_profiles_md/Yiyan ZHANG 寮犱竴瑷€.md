### The most important thing you’ve learned (or known) about China

China is a country with deep cultural roots and a diverse history. China's history is filled with diverse cultures, ideologies, ethnicities, and political systems. This diversity has shaped its unique civilization. Because of this complexity, many countries around the world have incomplete understanding of China in many aspects and many Western ideas and norms cannot apply here without certain degree of modification.
